    How to delete reset codes

    Delete reset codes if you've forgotten them or think somebody else knows them. Reset codes are irreversibly encrypted so you can't look them up.

    • 1. Click "My account"
    • 2. Click "Reset codes"
    • 3. Click reset code to delete
    • 4. Click "Delete code"
    • 5. Submit form
    • 6. Success
