    How to delete billing profiles

    Old billing profiles can be deleted if they are not your default.

    • 1. Click "Subscriptions and billing"
    • 2. Click "Billing profiles"
    • 3. Click billing profile to delete
    • 4. Click "Delete"
    • 5. Submit form
    • 6. Success
