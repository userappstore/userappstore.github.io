    How to refund credits

    If you have received credit cancelling a subscription you can refund that credit.

    • 1. Click "Subscriptions and billing"
    • 2. Click "Credits"
    • 3. Click credit to refund
    • 4. Click "Refund balance"
    • 5. Select billing profile and submit form
    • 6. Success
