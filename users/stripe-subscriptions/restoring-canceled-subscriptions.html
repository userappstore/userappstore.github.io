    How to restore canceled subscriptions

    A subscription can be restored while it is marked for cancelation at the end of the billing period.

    • 1. Click "Subscriptions and billing"
    • 2. Click "Subscriptions"
    • 3. Click subscription to restore
    • 4. Click "Restore subscription"
    • 5. Submit form
    • 6. Success
