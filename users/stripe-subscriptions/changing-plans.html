    How to change subscription plans

    Switching from one plan to another may incur charges if there is an amount owed.

    • 1. Click "Subscriptions and billing"
    • 2. Click "Subscriptions"
    • 3. Click subscription to change plans for
    • 4. Click "Change plan"
    • 5. Select new plan and submit form
    • 6. Success
