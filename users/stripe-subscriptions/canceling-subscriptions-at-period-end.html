    How to cancel subscriptions at period end

    This allows you to continue using your subscription until the next billing period.

    • 1. Click "Subscriptions and billing"
    • 2. Click "Subscriptions"
    • 3. Click subscription to cancel
    • 4. Click "Cancel subscription"
    • 5. Select at period end and submit form
    • 6. Success
