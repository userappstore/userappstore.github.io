    How to pay invoices

    If an invoice can't pay automatically you can optionally update your payment information and pay it yourself.

    • 1. Click "Subscriptions and billing"
    • 2. Click "Invoices"
    • 3. Click invoice to pay
    • 4. Click "Pay invoice"
    • 5. Select billing profile and submit form
    • 6. Success
