    How to update company owners

    Company owners can be updated until the owner information is submitted.

    • 1. Click "Stripe Connect accounts"
    • 2. Click "Stripe accounts"
    • 3. Click registration to update owner
    • 4. Click "Company owners"
    • 5. Click owner to update
    • 6. Click "Edit"
    • 7. Submit form
    • 8. Success
