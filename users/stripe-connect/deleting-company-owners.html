    How to delete company owners

    If you have erroneously added a company owner they can be deleted prior to submission.

    • 1. Click "Stripe Connect accounts"
    • 2. Click "Stripe accounts"
    • 3. Click registration to delete owner for
    • 4. Click "Company owners"
    • 5. Click owner to delete
    • 6. Click "Delete"
    • 7. Submit form
    • 8. Success
