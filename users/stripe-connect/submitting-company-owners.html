    How to submit company owners

    If your company registration requires owner information you must submit your empty-or-completed owners.

    • 1. Click "Stripe Connect accounts"
    • 2. Click "Stripe accounts"
    • 3. Click registration to submit owners
    • 4. Click "Company owners"
    • 5. Click "Submit completed"
    • 6. Submit form
    • 7. Success
