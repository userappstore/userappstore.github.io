    How to transfer organizations

    Transferring an organization makes someone else the owner.

    • 1. Click "Manage organizations"
    • 2. Click "Organizations"
    • 3. Click organization to transfer
    • 4. Click "Transfer ownership"
    • 5. Select new owner and submit form
    • 6. Success
