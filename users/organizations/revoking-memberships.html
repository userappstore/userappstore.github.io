    How to revoke memberships

    This removes a member from an organization.

    • 1. Click "Manage organizations"
    • 2. Click "Organizations"
    • 3. Click organization to revoke membership
    • 4. Click "Memberships"
    • 5. Click membership to delete
    • 6. Click "Delete"
    • 7. Submit form
    • 8. Success
