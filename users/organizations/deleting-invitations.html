    How to delete invitations

    Delete an organization's unused invitations.

    • 1. Click "Manage organizations"
    • 2. Click organization to delete invitation for
    • 3. Click "Invitations"
    • 4. Click invitation to delete
    • 5. Click "Delete invitation"
    • 6. Submit form
    • 7. Success
