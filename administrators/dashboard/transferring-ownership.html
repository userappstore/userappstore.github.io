    How to transfer ownership

    The application owner can transfer ownership to another account and be reduced to an administrator.

    • 1. Click "Dashboard administration"
    • 2. Click "Accounts"
    • 3. Click account to transfer ownership to
    • 4. Click "Transfer ownership"
