    How to add administrator access

    Add administrators when more people need access to the administration interface or APIs. Only the owner account can add administrators.

    • 1. Click "Dashboard administration"
    • 2. Click "Accounts"
    • 3. Click account to make administrator
    • 4. Click "Assign administrator"
    • 5. Submit form
    • 6. Success
