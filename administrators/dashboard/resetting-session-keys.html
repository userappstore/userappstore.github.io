    How to reset session keys

    Resetting an account's session key immediately invalidates all prior sessions.

    • 1. Click "Dashboard administration"
    • 2. Click "Accounts"
    • 3. Click account to end sessions
    • 4. Click "Reset session key"
    • 5. Submit form
    • 6. Success
