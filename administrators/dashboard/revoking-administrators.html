    How to revoke administrator access

    Removing administration rights ends that persons access to the administration interface and APIs.

    • 1. Click "Dashboard administration"
    • 2. Click "Administrators"
    • 3. Click administrator to revoke access
    • 4. Click "Revoke administrator"
    • 5. Revoke administrator access
    • 6. Account is no longer an administrator
