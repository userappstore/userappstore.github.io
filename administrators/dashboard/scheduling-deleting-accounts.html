    How to schedule deleting accounts

    Deleting accounts after a cooling-off period allows the accounts to be restored.

    • 1. Click "Dashboard administration"
    • 2. Click "Accounts"
    • 3. Click account to schedule deleting
    • 4. Click "Schedule account deleting"
    • 5. Schedule deleting
    • 6. Account is scheduled for deleting
